OUR STORY

Welcome to OneByte Foods, where culinary excellence meets technological innovation. Situated in the heart of Kathmandu city, OneByte Foods is not just a restaurant; it's a testament to our passion for great food and seamless dining experiences.
Founded with a vision to merge the rich flavors of Nepali cuisine with modern dining conveniences, OneByte Foods was born out of a desire to elevate your dining experience. What started as a humble endeavor has now blossomed into a premier dining destination, beloved by locals and visitors alike.
At OneByte Foods, we understand the importance of creating memorable moments around the dining table. That's why we've embarked on a journey to revolutionize the way you experience dining with our cutting-edge dine-in booking system and efficient management tools.
